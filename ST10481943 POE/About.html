About Us

Providing lifelong support and development for writers

Since January 1989, we have been committed to helping writers develop their craft and confidently navigate the often complex world of publishing. Over the years, we have supported thousands of aspiring authors by offering expert guidance, practical training, and a welcoming environment where creativity thrives.

Our programme offers a wide range of writing courses designed for everyone — from complete beginners to seasoned writers looking to refine their technique. Regardless of your age or educational background, our structured learning path supports you at every stage of your writing journey.

All our tutors are professional writers with real industry experience. They offer personalised feedback, share practical insights, and help you strengthen your voice as a storyteller while developing a strong command of writing fundamentals.

In addition to our expert tutors, our dedicated student advisors and administrative team work hard to build a warm, friendly, and supportive learning atmosphere. We believe every writer deserves a safe space to experiment, learn, and grow — and that is what makes our community so special.

Whether your dream is to publish a novel, improve your creative writing skills, or simply explore a new passion, we are here to guide and support you every step of the way. Your story matters, and we look forward to being part of your writing journey.
